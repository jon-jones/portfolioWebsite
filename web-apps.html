Pigs Card Game

Click Here to Play

This is a card game where you take turns rolling the dice to gain points and the first to 100 wins!

Rules:

The player rolls the dice, gaining round points for whatever they roll.
If you roll a single 1 or two 6s, your turn is over and you lose your points.
You must press hold to save your points and end your turn for the round.
The first to 100, or the user set value wins!

Notes:

This game was made using javascript and through a course done by Jonas Schmedtmann

Retro Simon Game

Click Here to Play

This is a retro version of Simon Says

Rules:

The player is given a color pattern, and they must reciprocate the pattern
After each round the color pattern grows by one
The goal is to beat your high score of how many rounds you complete!

Notes:

This game was made using JQuery and through a course done by Angela Yu
I personally added the functionality of high score and displaying the full pattern display instead of just the newest color
